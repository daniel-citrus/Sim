How to Play
  • The first player draws a straight line between any two vertices in their color.
  • The second player draws a straight line between any two vertices in their color.
  • Take turns placing lines until one player completes a triangle with all three sides in their color.
  • The first person to create a triangle, LOSES!
  • Examples
    All three vertices of a triangle must be on the outer edge of the diagram.
